Charlotte “Charlie” Fisk Is a student in the Multimedia Production program. They enjoy escape rooms because working through puzzles with a group makes both a fun bonding activity and is a fun exercise in critical thinking, and problem solving. They also enjoy design (Specifically character and prop design) a skill that they’re happy to improve on in design classes in the course. During their formative schooling years, they were regarded by teachers as the creative (and nondisruptive) one of their classes. They have helped with a mural for an elementary school and done several years of volunteer work at their local library. Charlie has three siblings and is the middle child among them. Their hobbies include Art, Video games, and scrolling on their phone for hours at a time. Some of their favorite music artists include Freddie Mercury, Gotye, Autoheart, and many Vocaloid artists (Flavor Folley, Jamie Page, SAWTOWNE, etc.…) Their chosen place to travel if ever given the chance would be Venice and the surrounding Italian cities. Outside of school they are slowly but surely working on several creative projects including several Comics, Books, and a pitch for an animated TV show. Many inspirations of theirs are animated tv shows like Gravity Falls, Over The Garden Wall, and The Owl House. Alongside some smaller (But no less impressive) indie cartoons including Murder Drones, I Don’t Want To Be A Magical Girl, and a more recent release Knights Of Guinevere.
